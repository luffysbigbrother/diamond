# Diamond

A daily MLB series prediction game.

Every week MLB teams play 2, 3, and 4-game series. Diamond lets you call how each one plays out — game by game — before the first pitch is thrown. Come back each day to see how your read is holding up, then lock in your updated predictions for the games still to come.

---

## How to Play

**Before the series starts**
Pick a winner for every game in the series. Think it's a sweep? Pick the same team all the way through. Think it splits? Call it how you see it. Lock in your picks before first pitch.

**The next day**
Yesterday's result is revealed — your pick on that game is painted green if you called it right, red if you didn't. The remaining games are open. You can stick with your original read or adjust based on what you just watched. Lock in again.

**This repeats every day until the series ends.**

The story of how your read evolved — game by game, day by day — is visible right on the card. You can see where you held firm, where you switched, and whether it paid off.

---

## Scoring

Picking winners early is worth more than picking them late. Sticking with a correct read through the whole series earns bonus points. Switching your pick and still getting it wrong costs you.

At the end of each series, bonus points are awarded if you called the overall series outcome correctly on Day 1 — sweep, split, or the exact win-loss record.

Your weekly score and accuracy rate are tracked at the top of the app.

---

## What You See

Each series shows up as a card with both teams, their current records, and the starting pitchers for each game. As the week goes on, results paint in and new pick rows open up below. The card tells the whole story of the series in one view.

---

## Notes

- The app pulls live MLB schedule and standings data automatically
- Picks are saved in your browser and persist between sessions
- The app refreshes itself at midnight so morning reveals happen automatically
- Works on desktop and mobile
